Sportsbettingonline.ag announces NBA Finals Insurance Policy



  Tweet

Whether Golden State or Cleveland win the 2014-15 NBA Championship, SBO introduced some levies in case there’s a sweep


San Jose, Costa Rica – June 3, 2015 – It's always good to have a Plan B just in case – especially in sports betting. That's why SportsBettingOnline.ag wants to provide bettors with a little insurance as the NBA Finals approach.

As the debate continues to rage on as to whether the Golden State Warriors or Cleveland Cavaliers will win the 2014-15 NBA Championship, SBO has introduced some levies in case there's a sweep. The diligent team of risk managers at SBO has crunched the numbers and determined that it's nearly impossible that the finals will be a sweep one way or the other, but just in case, they've initiated a promotion to protect bettors if the unexpected does happen.

"We're expecting a tight series that lasts at least five games but likely six or seven,” said Dave Johnson, the head oddsmaker at SportsBettingOnline.ag. “However, if anyone does bet the series price on either team and the series does end in a four game sweep, we'll refund all losing bets”.

The latest odds show that the Golden State Warriors are a -240 favorite in the series while the Cleveland Cavaliers are a +200 underdog. The action gets underway on Thursday night when the Cavs visit Oakland for Game 1 at Oracle Arena.

The sweep refund is a great gesture and one that bettors are likely to appreciate – especially after the conference finals. “We saw a couple of very hotly contested conference finals in the NHL the past couple of weeks but the NBA conference finals were not very competitive”, said Johnson. The Warriors nearly swept the Houston Rockets, taking care of business in five games, while the Cavaliers swept the Atlanta Hawks.

So if you're planning to bet the NBA finals series price, your best bet is to do it at SportsBettingOnline.ag. If you win your bet, you're in great shape but remember that if the team you bet on gets swept in the NBA final, SBO will refund all losing bets for the full amount.

It might seem odd for a sports betting site to refund losing bets. After all, the very nature of sportsbooks is to pay out winning bets and keep losing ones. But paying players back for losses is commonplace at SportsBettingOnline.ag.

The online sportsbook has paid players back for bets that were seemingly in the bag but never materialized. The site has also reimbursed players for controversial calls that resulted in losing bets.

But it's not just getting paid for losing bets that makes SportsBettingOnline.ag a fan-favorite. The online sportsbook also pays players cash for losing bets.
