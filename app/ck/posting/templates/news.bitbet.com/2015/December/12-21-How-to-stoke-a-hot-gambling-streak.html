How to stoke a hot gambling streak

As any gambler will tell you, there are hot streaks and there are cold streaks.


As any gambler will tell you, there are hot streaks and there are cold streaks. The best players have more hot than cold. Keeping a hot streak burning isn’t just up to the gambling gods. Rather, smart players know tips and tactics to make the most of these runs and hold the cold at bay. Here’s how you ride a hot streak when you are bitcoin gambling – no matter what game you’re playing:

“Don’t get cocky”

Han Solo wasn’t just talking about shooting down space ships when he uttered those famous words. And he was a pretty seasoned gambler, running with the likes of Lando Calrissian. The worst thing you can do is disrespect the hot streak.

That can be anything from wagering too much to just losing focus, especially in some skill-based games like blackjack or poker. Constantly defying the odds can make you feel invincible, but you shouldn’t act like it.

Bet smart
Some betting strategies are based on milking as much profit from a hot streak as possible, such as the progressive system. As you win, you double your wager each time, so a $10 winner is a $20 bet next time, and if that wins then you wager $40 next round.

It is a preferred system for those who can afford it, because it can become quite costly when that hot streak comes to an end. For those of more modest means, don’t be lured into wagering more than you should by a hot streak. You got here playing within your limits, so be safe and stick to them.

Know when to bail
Perhaps even more important than recognizing a heater is recognizing when things are about to turn sour. Most bettors will have a cut-off point on winnings, shooting for an exact number or looking for a certain percentage of return on investment. When you obtain those goals and surpass them, don’t get too greedy and try to hit the big score.

Behind every hot streak are expanding mathematical contradictions, as you continue to defy the odds or house edge. Over time, those variants to return to the norm – AKA cool off. A hot streak is only a true hot streak if you walk away with more in your pocket, so know when enough is enough.
