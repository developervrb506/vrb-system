Get to know your pokers



  Tweet

Here’s a sample of each to help you find your perfect poker match


Bitcoin Poker is arguably the most popular casino game with endless variations and twists to the game. BitBet.com recognizes the demand for great poker games among our casino players and offers a massive menu of poker options. Here’s a sample of each to help you find your perfect poker match:

Casino Hold’Em
Not to be confused with Texas Hold’Em, Casino Hold’Em is also known as Caribbean Hold’Em. Players start the game with an ante bet and are dealt two cards face down, along with the dealer. Then three cards are dealt to the table as community cards. Players then have the option to fold, losing their initial ante bet, or continue going forward by placing a call bet of double the ante. Two more cards are dealt to the table and whoever has the best five card hand is the winner.

Tri-Card Poker
Also known as three-card poker, Tri-Card Poker starts with an ante bet and three cards dealt to the players and dealer face down. Players have the option to fold and lose their original ante bet or call, placing a bet of double the initial ante wager. Cards are exposed and whoever has the best three-card poker hand wins. However, there are bonus for high-ranked poker hands such as pairs, three of a kind, straights, flushes and straight flushes – all with set payouts.

Let It Ride
A great name for a great game. Unlike some of the other poker variations above, this one doesn’t involve beating the dealer but rather trying to hit a high-ranked poker hand – all of which have payouts assigned to them. Let It Ride poker begins with an ante bet and players are dealt three cards. At this point, you can choose to “Let It Ride” and continue and even raise the stakes if you think you have a solid hand. The player is given a single face-up card and can once again choose to “Let It Ride” in order to see the fifth and final card.

Video Poker
In the same vein as “Let It Ride”, most video poker games have assigned payouts to high-ranking poker hands. Often times, there are assigned Wild Cards that can help players fill in the holes for a winning hand. The most popular video poker games include Jacks or Better, Deuces Wild, and Aces and Faces. These are great games that any player can pick up quick and enjoy at any time.
