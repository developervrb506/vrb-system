Two AL Teams Offering Good Value On The World Series Futures



  Tweet

That’s why these two teams could be worth a value play


 

We’re approaching the 2015 MLB All-Star Break, so now is a good time to bet sportsbook futures in terms of World Series winners.  While the National League has some clear-cut favorites, the American League is wide open as of now. That’s why these two teams could be worth a value play:

Toronto Blue Jays (+1000):
The Blue Jays might be the toughest team in all of baseball to handicap. They're holding their own in the American League East, but no one in that division is particularly very good. They lead the New York Yankees in runs scored by almost 60 (the top three run-scoring teams in the majors come from the AL East with Baltimore ranking third), but they're 24th in ERA and dead last in saves. They have to make a move before the trade deadline to shore up their pitching, especially the bullpen, and it might be a stretch to think that they can continue to drive in runs at this rate. However, stranger things have happened and if they make the right moves, the Blue Jays can be an excellent value pick in terms of World Series odds.

Los Angeles Angels (+2000):
Like their National League counterparts, the Angels have a ton of talent on paper that should be enough to get them to the postseason because no one expects Houston to continue their play. Strangely, it has been the offense that has struggled as the Angels are 20th in runs scored, while their pitching hasn't been great, but they're 12th in ERA and that is with Jered Weaver struggling. They also have a fantastic manager in Mike Scioscia, who can be the difference between winning and losing a few games. The offense should come around eventually, and as long as the pitching holds up, the Angels could shoot up the power rankings very quickly in an American League that might be looking to catch up to the National League. If you’re looking to bet sportsbook odds with the Angels, you should get about 20/1 on them to win the World Series, which is great value.
