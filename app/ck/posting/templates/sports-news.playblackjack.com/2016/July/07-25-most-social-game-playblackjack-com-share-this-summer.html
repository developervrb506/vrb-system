Social games at PlayBlackjack.com to share this summer


Summer is a time for friends and family, so whether you’re having a friendly game of blackjack at the cottage, part of a crew hitting up your local casino, or introducing your team to PlayBlackjack.com, these games seem to be better with a crowd:

6-deck Blackjack
To us, nothing beats a packed blackjack table. The comradery of players versus the dealer brings you all together, with cheers and jeers being shared across the cards. Some players like a solitary game, if they’re focusing hard on a card-counting system. But with 6-deck blackjack you can play multiple hands, and enjoy a more leisurely pace which allows your bankroll to last a little longer.

Switch Blackjack
On its own, Switch Blackjack is a blast. The ability to swap out cards to make two better hands versus the dealer makes it an interesting twist on the standard 21. However, when you throw a couple extra hands into the mix, it becomes even more fun as high-ranked cards become a premium with so many on the table. Another great thing about Switch Blackjack is that you have double the shot of winning, which makes for a high-stakes, high-pace game that thrills everyone involved.

Craps
While we specialize in blackjack, it’s not all we do. We also give tips on how to play craps. Casino players can find their favorites beyond the blackjack table, and no game is more social than craps. If you’ve ever been inside a casino – whether it was 6 in the morning or 6 at night – the loudest spot it always the craps table. Bet on or against the shooter, making instant friends and enemies, or try your hand at some of the inside bets for big paydays.

Pai Gow Poker
This variation on poker is gaining popularity among casino patrons, with a great social aspect and longer playing time. Pai Gow Poker forces players to make two poker hands against the dealer and must win both hands in order to pay out. It makes for plenty of pushes, but if you’re just out for a night of gambling fun and looking to stretch you bankroll, Pai Gow is a great social game.
