Delta Downs Jackpot horse racing betting preview



  Tweet

Here’s a look at the betting favorites before you start betting on horse races


 

Some of the finest two-year-old contenders in the country head to Vinton, Louisiana for the Delta Down Jackpot this Saturday. The 1 1/16-mile – 8.5 furlong – event is a precursor to the 2016 Triple Crown season, with some of these horses making their way to Churchill Downs in the spring. Here’s a look at the betting favorites for Saturday’s race before you start betting on horse races:

Exaggerator (+200)
This Keith Desormeaux-trained horse is among the early hopefuls for the Kentucky Derby, priced at 30/1 odds to win the Run for the Rose in the first weekend in June. He’s coming off a fourth-place showing in the Breeders' Cup Juvenile at the end of October and placed second at the Breeders Futurity earlier this month. He won the Saratoga Special in August and took a first place win at Delmar in July. He went back to California to train following the Breeders’ Cup and looked good in training session at San Anita Park last weekend.

Sunny Ridge (+350)
Sunny Ridge didn’t take part in the Breeders’ Cup but did finish runner-up at the Champagne at Belmont Park in October and won the Sapling at Monmouth Park back in September. The Jason Servis-trained horse had the lead in the Champagne but crumbled down the stretch, losing by 4.5 lengths to Greenpointcrusader. Now, he faces the longest race of his career. Will he have enough in the tank to contend as the second overall favorite?

Forevamo (+400)
Forevamo is showing great value this weekend, having already run at Delta Downs this season. He won the Jean Lafitte Stakes there in October, which is a tuneup race for the Jackpot. Forevamo is the offspring of Uncle Mo, who was amazing as a two-year-old winning the Champagne and the Breeders' Cup Juvenile back in 2010. The one thing going against Forevamo is that he drew the No. 1 post for Saturday’s race and has run from the outside in his three 2015 events. But this horse has shown fight, especially during his win at Delta Downs, battling back after he was challenged down the stretch.
